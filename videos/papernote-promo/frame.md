# PaperNote Promo Design System

## Canvas

- 1920×1080, 16:9
- Background: paper white `#F7FBFA` with soft mint gradients
- Safe margin: 96 px

## Brand

- Primary: `#0F766E`
- Dark ink: `#102A2A`
- Mint: `#DDF5F1`
- Secondary blue: `#3B82F6`
- Muted text: `#54706E`

## Typography

- Chinese: Microsoft YaHei / system sans-serif
- Latin brand: Segoe UI / system sans-serif
- Headlines: 72–110 px, bold
- Supporting text: 30–44 px
- Labels: 22–30 px

## Visual Language

- Rounded white product cards with restrained teal borders and soft shadows
- Thin grid-paper accents and hand-drawn ink strokes
- Real product screenshots remain readable and are never replaced with invented UI
- Transitions use short upward reveals, gentle scale, masked wipes and line drawing
- No photorealistic decoration, neon glow or excessive 3D effects
